# Berita2600 in Word Cloud (Python Notebook)

Delving into [Social Media (Instagram) Content of Pusdiklat BPS](https://www.instagram.com/pusdiklat.bps) in 2023

> Presented for my former PR team in January 21, 2024

## Background
- Think of “Berita2600” as our treasure trove of content, built up piece by piece throughout 2023.
- After all that effort, I just couldn't resist the urge to dig deeper and see what gems were hidden inside.
- So, I grabbed some handy word clouds and charts to get a feel for the most frequent words. 
- Let's see where this adventure takes us!

## Objectives
1. Understand the key themes of social media contents of Pusdiklat BPS in 2023
2. Identify potential improvements for 2024

## Methodology
1. Scrap our blog with [simplescraper](https://simplescraper.io/)
2. Clean the data with [spreadsheets](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/13hsXm20S6RdAyK520rLLkbbUJEywRp5F/edit?usp=sharing&ouid=101051388151123974532&rtpof=true&sd=true)
3. Create word cloud & charts with python via [google colab](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1h-E5dHt6ZV-QrZE3D2jbWYKjRjUgBT1MAUFNKhDOsP4/edit?usp=sharing)
3. Craft insights & recommendations
